# KCS Drills
### Kinetic Combat System Drill Generator

A martial arts drill generator for MSi, Lameco, and Ilustrisimo systems.  Use Visual-Spatial learning to help student memorize and mastere the drill used in these sytem relying on images, colors, diagrams, and text layouts rather than just plain spoken words.  This is your digital instructor.

## How to Install on Your Phone

### Android (Samsung, Google Pixel, etc.)
1. Open **Chrome**
2. Go to **https://kcs-drills.netlify.app**
3. Tap the **three dots** in the top right corner
4. Tap **Install app**
5. Tap **Install**
6. Find **KCS Drills** in your app drawer

### iPhone / iPad
1. Open **Safari** — must be Safari, not Chrome
2. Go to **https://kcs-drills.netlify.app**
3. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
5. Tap **Add**
6. Find **KCS Drills** on your home screen

---

## How to Use

1. **Select system** — MSi (15 angles), Lameco (12 angles), or Ilustrisimo (12 angles)
2. **Enter number of students** and **attacks per student**
3. **Select mode:**
   - **Known** — all student assignments shown at once on screen
   - **Secret** — enter student names, each student sees only their own angles revealed one at a time
4. **New drill** — reruns with same settings but fresh random angles
5. **Back to start** — resets everything
